How can we help?

Find answers to common questions about TARA

Getting Started
How do I create an account?
Click Register on the login page. Fill in your full name, username, password, business name, business password, industry, and business size. After registering, you will receive a unique Business ID. Share this with your team members so they can join your business account.
What is a Business ID?
Your Business ID is a unique code (e.g., TARA-ABCD-1234) generated when you register. All team members use this ID along with the business password to log in and access the same business data. You can find it in your Profile Settings.
How do I add team members?
Go to Profile → Team Management. Click "Add User" and fill in their details. You can assign roles: Admin (full access), Manager (can view reports, manage transactions), or Cashier (can only record income). Available on Pro and Enterprise plans.
What plans are available?
TARA offers three plans:

Basic (₱500/month) — Cash Management, Sales, General Journal, Business Insights.
Pro (₱1,500/month) — All Basic features + AR, AP, Inventory, Audit Log, up to 10 users.
Enterprise (₱5,000/month) — All Pro features + Multi-branch, API Access, BIR Integration, priority support.
Managing Finances
How do I record a sale?
Go to Sales Management. You can record a sale manually by entering the customer name, amount, and description, or select a product from your inventory. The sale will automatically update your cash balance and inventory.
How do I track expenses?
Go to Cash Management. Click "Add Transaction", select "Expense", enter the description, amount, and category. All expenses are tracked and visible in your Business Insights.
How do I create an invoice?
Go to Accounts Receivable. First add a customer, then create an invoice by selecting the customer, entering the amount, and setting a due date. You can record partial or full payments against the invoice.
How do purchase orders work?
Go to Purchase Orders. Create an order by selecting a supplier, adding items with quantities and prices. When you receive the items, click "Receive" — this automatically updates your inventory and creates an Accounts Payable bill.
How do sales orders work?
Go to Sales Orders. Create an order for a customer with items and quantities. When you deliver, click "Deliver" — this deducts inventory and automatically creates an Accounts Receivable invoice.
Features
How does the Predictive Planner work?
Go to Business Insights → Predictive Planner. Adjust variables like price, volume, and costs. The system calculates projected profit in real-time. You can save scenarios and load them later to compare different strategies.
How do I import data from CSV?
Go to Profile → Import Data. Select the module (Cash, Sales, Inventory, Customers, or Suppliers), download the template, fill in your data, and upload. The system validates each row and imports valid records.
How do I generate a receipt?
Receipts are available for sales, income transactions, and AR payments. Click the receipt icon next to any record. A professional receipt opens with your business name, customer details, and VAT breakdown. Click "Print" to save as PDF.
What reports can I export?
Every table in TARA has an Export CSV button. You can export your Cash transactions, Sales, Invoices, Bills, Products, Journal, and Ledger data as CSV files that open in Excel or Google Sheets.
Account & Settings
How do I change my plan?
Go to Profile → Billing & Plan. You can view your current plan and upgrade options. Contact support to switch plans.
How do I switch to dark mode?
Go to Profile → Appearance and toggle the Dark Mode switch. Your preference is saved automatically.
How do I restore deleted items?
Go to Profile → Restore Data. You can view all deleted transactions, sales, invoices, bills, and products. Click "Restore" to recover any item.
How do VAT and taxes work?
Go to Profile → Tax Registration to set your business as VAT-Registered (12% VAT) or Non-VAT. Your receipts will automatically show the correct tax breakdown based on this setting.
